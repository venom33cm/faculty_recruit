Welcome to our Faculty Recruitment Service

In today's world, it is very hard to trace and find the right person to recruit, when the existing faculty retires. That too in a college, the management may face a hard time finding the perfect teaching staff. This situation can be handled by creating a tracking application that tracks the current faculty's working period. When the current faculty is about to retire, our application will alert the college about this. The college can then start tracing for a new faculty in advance. By the time the current faculty retires, there is a very good chance that the college can find their successor.
